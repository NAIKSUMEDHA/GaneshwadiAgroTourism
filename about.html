About Us

Welcome to Ganeshwadi Agri Tourism Center, founded by Ar. Aniruddha Naik, the visionary behind Aurangabad's first agri-tourism initiative. Nestled in the serene village of Undangaon, our center offers a tranquil escape amidst nature's embrace. Ganeshwadi Agri Tourism Center serves as a gateway to experience the essence of rural life, sustainable farming practices, and cultural immersion. Join us to explore lush green landscapes, interact with local farmers, and indulge in farm-to-table dining experiences featuring fresh, locally sourced ingredients.
